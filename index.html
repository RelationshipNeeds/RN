A GPS for Lasting Love

Backed by Roger Birkman’s groundbreaking research, Relationship Needs reveals 10 proven shortcuts and 188 tips to stay in love.

Why is it so hard to stay in love?

We can be doing all the right things. Loving each other. Spending time. Communicating—and really trying.

And still, we can get off track without meaning to. We may not be falling apart, but we’re not growing closer, either.

Why do our best intentions still lead to fallouts or distance between us?

We don’t want to fall out but something gets lost— between what we mean and what they hear.

How does that even happen?

Why do some couples grow closer—while others fall apart?

We all know couples who stay close...who support each other...still laugh and reach for each other.

How do they make it work?

What do happy couples know that we don't?

Wouldn't it be great if someone could research that? Well it turns out that one insightful psychologist did. His name was Dr. Roger Birkman and he decided to ask, 'what helps happy couples stay together?'

Not what’s broken—but, 'what works, and how can we all benefit from it?'

To answer this question, his breakthrough research observed happy couples who had been together for ten years or more. He listened and watched.

And what he discovered surprised everyone.

The secret to lasting love isn’t more effort. It’s better directions.

What Dr. Birkman discovered is now called our Behavioral Needs.

They’re the specific ways we each need to be treated—in communicating, socializing, making decisions and plans, sharing rewards, dealing with feelings...ten behavioral areas in all.

It turns out that happy couples aren’t more perfect. They’ve just figured out exactly how to meet each other’s Needs in everyday moments.

And now the secrets of a happy relationship that Dr. Birkman found are available for us all.
